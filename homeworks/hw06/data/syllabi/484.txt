EECS 484 provides a basic introduction to relational database management systems (DBMSs). By the end of the course, you will know how to use and implement a DBMS. Among other topics, we will cover SQL, relational algebra, query excecution, optimization, and ACID semantics.

When emailing course staff, please use 484questions@umich.edu with "EECS484" in the Subject.
